Equation Solver

This calculator allows you to enter equations with parentheses, exponents, multiplication, division, addition, and subtraction.

There are a few rules for entering your equation:

  • Valid operands are +, -, *, /, and ^
  • You must have spaces between numbers and operands, for example: 2 + 3 not 2+3
  • Format exponents like this: 5 ^ 2 or 2 ^ -1
  • Format expressions with parentheses like this: (-7 + 3) * (2 - 3)
  • You cannot have variables, letters, or two-sided equations. Letters and unsupported characters will be escaped out
  • Decimals and negative numbers are ok. Format negative numbers like this: -4
  • The calculator will follow order of operation (PEMDAS)
  • If the equation is not valid or cannot be handled, NaN (Not a Number) will be returned as the answer
